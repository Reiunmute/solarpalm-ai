# SolarPalm AI website design tokens

## Reference direction

Use Tesla solar panel pages as a layout reference: full-screen product photography, centered hero copy, restrained navigation, large quiet type, and simple rounded CTAs. Do not copy Tesla assets, text, logos, or brand marks.

## Product stance

SolarPalm AI should feel like a polished consumer energy product for Fiji. The page should look calm, premium, and installation-ready, while still keeping traceable numbers visible.

## Visual direction

- Hero: full viewport photo of solar installers, dark gradient overlay, centered headline and CTAs.
- Sections: alternating left/right large photography and text panels.
- Tone: warm sun, clean rooflines, black panels, white space, restrained typography.
- Motion: minimal. Prefer smooth hover and subtle reveal over decorative animation.
- Density: less dashboard, more product brochure.

## Type

- System sans only.
- H1: large, centered, confident, no cramped letter spacing.
- H2: clean product copy, not technical documentation.
- Numbers: tabular, but set inside calm product cards.

## Components

- Top nav: slim, fixed, transparent over hero, glass only after scroll is not needed for this static slice.
- Hero CTA: two pill buttons, one dark and one light.
- Product sections: 50/50 image and text on desktop. Stack on mobile.
- Calculator: integrated product panel, not a dark dashboard.
- Source strip: short proof cards, not a table.

## Anti-patterns

- Busy grid backgrounds.
- Neon or cyber dashboard effects.
- Developer README layout.
- Overly tiny cards.
- Claims that SolarPalm lends money or makes credit decisions.
